World Heritage Sites by year

I also created a map showing the location of the UNESCO World Heritage Sites 2019 but color coded by their year of inscription.
Some trends are easier to find this way, for example that in the Middle East and Far East Asia there are almost no sites inscribed during the first decade of the Heritage list, but there are several sites inscribed during the last decades.

Repository: github.com/sergioisla/world_heritage_sites



Public Wifi access points in Mexico City

This map shows wifi access according to open data from Mexico City government. Some coordinates had errors and were located outside from the city, but the mistakes were easy to identify and allowed easy corrections directly in the csv file.

Most of the populated area of the city is covered with wifi spots, except:
-Few wealthy neighborhoods with low population density because there are very big houses.
-Some neighborhoods that are not open to public transit.

Repository: github.com/sergioisla/public_wifi_cdmx
